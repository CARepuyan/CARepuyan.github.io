Hundered Islands, Pangasinan

Hunderd Island epitomizes the best of Pangasinan, riddled by 123 islets spearing out of cerulean sea. Home of a staggering variety of wildlife and fishes, these coral islands make up the Hundered Islands National Park. Most of them deslate, save for the seven major islands that are usually included in the Island Hopping tour: Quezon Island, Governor’s Island, Marcos Island, Children’s Island, Virgin Island, Pilgrimage/Ramos Island (the one with large Jesus statue) and Scouts Island. Sabang Beach, Baler, Aurora

Surfing in the Philippines began in Baler. This small surfing town is located in the central part of the Aurora province, east of Luzon, and is a popular destination for travelers because of its sensational waves. Baler presents a quaint and laid-back vibe suitable for those looking for cozy beach resorts near Manila. Through the years, Baler has maintained its place, being one of the top attractions in the country. Aside from being a surfing destination, it is also home to historical attractions and breathtaking landscapes. Read More
